Our Mission

At Eyes Outside Aviation, our mission is to provide exceptional flight training that empowers aspiring pilots to achieve their aviation dreams. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of safety, professionalism, and educational excellence.

Our Values

  • Safety First: Safety is our top priority in all operations
  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in training and service
  • Community: We foster a supportive aviation community
  • Growth: We support continuous learning and improvement

Our Facilities

Our state-of-the-art facilities include modern classrooms, flight planning areas, and a fleet of well-maintained aircraft. We provide students with access to the latest aviation technology and training materials to ensure the best possible learning experience.
