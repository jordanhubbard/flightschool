Our History

Founded in 1995, {{ config.SCHOOL_NAME }} has been a cornerstone of aviation training in the region. What started as a small flight school with a single aircraft has grown into a premier training facility with a fleet of modern aircraft and a team of experienced instructors.

Over the years, we've trained thousands of pilots who have gone on to successful careers in both commercial and private aviation. Our commitment to excellence and safety has earned us recognition as one of the leading flight schools in the country.
